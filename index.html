About Me

Hello! I am Aisyah Jihan Ulayya, a student of Fine Arts - Visual Communication Design concentration at Universitas Negeri Semarang.

I have a deep interest in digital illustration, character design, and the development of communicative and aesthetically appealing visual elements. My main focus is to create visual works that are fun, expressive, and have strong characters, while still maintaining the value of professionalism and the function of design communication.

Through an explorative and concept-based approach, I enjoy exploring the combination of a playful visual style with a clear visual message objective. I am also active in deepening my design skills through various personal, academic, and collaborative projects-both in print, digital, and interactive media.
